Our Mission

Books at Every Doorstep

Ketabe Damavand is a beloved bookstore in Tehran. We run a book subscription program that delivers curated, age-appropriate books to children across Iran every month.

We work with NGOs to identify children in need ‚Äî many from families who cannot afford books on their own. Your donation makes it possible for a child to receive a package of hand-picked books, sparking curiosity and building a lifelong love of reading.

Why Books?

Giving a book to a child is more than a simple gift

When we gift a book to a child, it's as if we're opening a window to the world for them. A window through which they can see faraway lands, meet new people, and experience imagination without limits. Every book is an invitation to discover ‚Äî to ask, to see, and to think.

A book is not just a few pages of paper; it's a path that gradually takes shape in a child's mind and grows with them. Stories teach them that the world is bigger than their limited everyday experiences. They come to understand that people are different, cultures are colorful, and every subject can find meaning from a different angle. This breadth of perspective takes root years later in their choices and behaviors.

A generation that grows up with books builds a different society. People who read ask more questions, think more deeply, and empathize more easily. These are the very roots upon which a healthy and humane society stands.

Perhaps in these days, when worries are many and the world seems unstable, gifting books to children takes on a deeper meaning. We may not be able to change everything at once, but we can take small, steady steps. Every book is a seed planted in a child's mind ‚Äî a seed that may one day grow into a tree of awareness, kindness, and responsibility.

Change is not always sudden and loud. Sometimes it begins from the heart of a simple story and quietly takes root in a person's outlook and decisions. If today we read to children and gift them books, we are giving hope to the future ‚Äî hope for a more aware, kinder, and brighter world.
